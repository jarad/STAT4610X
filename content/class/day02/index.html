Find another individual, who is not sitting next to you, and, ideally, who you don’t know. The less you know the individual the better.

Introduce yourselves by stating your name and your favorite statistics course (STAT 4610X is not an option).

The categories chosen will be specific to sports analytics. As a reminder, the term sports is being used very broadly, i.e. so long as there has been at least one national or international championship it will qualify as a sport.

While we play this game, think about each category introduced by the instructor and how you might analyze data arising from that category. Analyses within a category will likely have very similar statistical methods used compared to analyses across categories.
